Company Policy ‚Äì Preksha Enterprises
At Preksha Enterprises, we believe in building trust through transparency, delivering excellence through experience, and maintaining lasting relationships through service. With over 25 years in the natural stone industry, we strive to be a responsible, quality-driven, and globally respected exporter of Indian sandstone and other natural stones.
1. Quality Assurance Policy

We are committed to providing natural stones that meet the highest standards in terms of aesthetics, durability, and finish. Our sandstone is sourced from the best quarries in Rajasthan and undergoes thorough inspection before dispatch. We follow a strict quality control process across selection, cutting, calibration, packaging, and loading. All products conform to international dimensional and physical property standards. Customized sizes, textures, and finishes are available on request. Final inspection is conducted before shipment to ensure zero defects.



2. Ethical Sourcing & Labor Policy

Preksha Enterprises is committed to ethical business practices. We ensure that: All our products are sourced from government-authorized and environmentally compliant quarries. We do not support or engage in any form of child labor, forced labor, or unfair labor practices. All workers and staff are treated with respect, paid fairly, and work in safe conditions.



3. Environmental Sustainability Policy

We understand the importance of protecting our environment for future generations. Our sustainability practices include: Reducing water waste and recycling water used in stone processing. Promoting the use of eco-friendly packaging materials. Partnering with quarries and suppliers who follow sustainable mining techniques.



4. Export, Packaging & Logistics Policy

We serve global clients with a smooth and reliable export process: Packaging is done using seaworthy, weather-resistant materials like wooden crates with polythene and foam lining. Each crate is labeled and barcoded for easy tracking and handling. We provide complete documentation including Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, and Bill of Lading. Delivery schedules are strictly maintained, with full communication throughout the logistics process.



5. Customer Service Policy

We believe that great service is as important as great stone. Our team ensures prompt responses to all inquiries. Orders are handled with care, transparency, and professionalism. We offer technical guidance and product recommendations as per project requirements. Post-sale support is available for logistics tracking, damage claims, and reordering.



6. Privacy & Data Security Policy

We respect your privacy and safeguard all personal and business information shared with us. We do not sell or disclose any client data to third parties without consent, except as required for logistics and regulatory compliance.



7. Dispute Resolution Policy

In the unlikely event of a dispute, we aim to resolve matters amicably and fairly. All issues regarding product quality, damage in transit, or delays must be reported within 7 days of delivery. We will evaluate each case and offer replacements, refunds, or solutions based on the evidence provided.
