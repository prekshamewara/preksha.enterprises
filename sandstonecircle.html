Sandstone Circle
A sandstone circle is a pre-cut circular arrangement of sandstone paving stones used as a decorative feature in outdoor landscaping. It typically consists of a central circular piece (called a "centre stone"), surrounded by concentric rings of wedge-shaped sandstone segments that form a complete circle when laid together.
Add a timeless focal point to your outdoor space with our beautifully crafted sandstone circles . Designed using premium-quality natural sandstone, these pre-cut circular paving kits bring elegance, symmetry, and character to gardens, patios, courtyards, and entranceways. Available in a range of earthy tones like Raj Green, Autumn Brown, and Kandla Grey, each sandstone circle is precision-cut for easy installation and long-lasting durability. Whether used as a centerpiece or integrated into larger paving layouts, sandstone circles offer a perfect blend of traditional charm and natural beauty.
