ABOUT US

The company is a trusted name in the natural stone industry, proudly serving global clients for over 25 years. Based near Udaipur, Rajasthan, we are mine owners and manufacturers specializing in premium quality sandstone and paving stones. Our journey began with a commitment to quality, reliability, and ethical sourcing. With decades of experience and deep roots in the stone-rich region of Bijoliya, we offer a wide range of natural stone products, including Kandla Grey, Buff, Raj Blend, Autumn Brown, and custom finishes tailored to market needs. We supply to landscaping companies, importers, and builders across the UK, Europe, and other international markets, offering consistent quality, competitive pricing, and timely deliveries. Our materials are ideal for patios, pathways, garden features, and architectural use. At Preksha Enterprises, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern processing techniques to deliver stones that stand the test of time—both in appearance and durability.
